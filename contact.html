FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the US. Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available at checkout. International orders vary by destination, typically 7–14 days.
What is your return policy?
We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition with all packaging. Simply email us or start a return through your account dashboard, and we'll provide a prepaid label.
Do you offer a warranty?
Yes. Every MAISON product comes with our Lifetime Craftsmanship Warranty. If a product fails due to a manufacturing defect, we will repair or replace it free of charge, for life.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International orders may be subject to local import duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.
Can I visit your showroom?
Absolutely. Our Lyon showroom is open Monday through Saturday, 10am–7pm, and Sunday 12–5pm. We recommend booking an appointment for a dedicated consultation by emailing hello@maison.com.
