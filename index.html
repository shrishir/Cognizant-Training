Our Philosophy

Crafted with fire,
rooted in season.

Ember & Oak was born from a singular obsession: what happens when you strip cooking back to its most primal element — fire. Our open-hearth kitchen draws on centuries of wood-fire tradition while celebrating the ingredients that thrive minutes from our door.

Every plate is a study in restraint. We let the heat do the talking, and the season write the story.
