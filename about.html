Our Story

Made with Hands. Lived with Love.

Since 2007, MAISON has worked with artisan makers worldwide to bring quietly extraordinary objects into everyday homes.

Who We Are

Eighteen Years of Thoughtful Making

MAISON was founded in 2007 by Clara and Michel Beaumont, two architects who believed the objects we live with shape how we feel at home. Frustrated by mass-produced furniture that looked fine but felt empty, they began sourcing directly from small workshops across Europe and Japan.

What started as a small showroom in Lyon grew into a global community of more than 300 artisan makers, united by a shared commitment: make things well, make them honestly, and make them to last.

"A well-made object is a quiet act of resistance against the disposable. It says: this matters, and so do you."
— Clara Beaumont, Co-Founder

Today, every product in our collection is personally vetted by our team. We visit every workshop, know every maker by name, and stand behind every piece with our lifetime craftsmanship warranty.

What We Believe

Our Values

Every decision we make is guided by a simple set of principles.

Sustainability
We source only from makers who use sustainable or reclaimed materials and ethical labor practices.
Craftsmanship
Every piece is touched by human hands. We believe skilled making is a form of respect for materials.
Fair Trade
We pay artisans fairly and build long-term relationships — not transactional supplier agreements.
Longevity
We only sell things built to last. If something breaks through no fault of your own, we'll fix it. Always.
